# Privacy Policy for Macrode

**Last Updated:** July 2, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Macrode ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Data Storage and Collection

Macrode is designed with privacy as a core principle. 
- **Local Storage:** All user data, including dietary logs, caloric intake, body weight, recipes, and daily metrics, is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- **No Accounts:** The App does not require you to create an account.
- **No External Servers:** We do not transmit your personal dietary or health data to any external servers or third-party databases. 

## 2. Apple HealthKit Integration

Macrode offers an optional integration with Apple's Health app (HealthKit).
- If you choose to enable this feature, the App can read and write nutritional and body metric data to your Health database.
- **Data Protection:** Data accessed from HealthKit is never used for marketing, advertising, or data mining purposes. 
- You can manage or revoke the App's access to HealthKit at any time via the settings in the Apple Health app.

## 3. Camera Usage

The App requires access to your device's camera strictly for the purpose of **barcode scanning** to retrieve nutritional data.
- Images captured by the camera are processed on-device (via VisionKit).
- We do not record, store, or transmit any photos or video feeds from your camera.

## 4. Third-Party Services

To provide nutritional data for scanned barcodes, the App queries the **Open Food Facts** API.
- Only the barcode number (UPC/EAN) is sent to Open Food Facts to fetch the corresponding product details.
- No personal user data is sent along with this request.
- Please refer to the [Open Food Facts Privacy Policy](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/terms-of-use) for more information on how they handle requests.

## 5. Analytics and Tracking

Macrode does **not** use third-party analytics trackers, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
